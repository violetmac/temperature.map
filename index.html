US Temperature Change Map

Temperature Trends from 1985 to 2025

Project Description

This visualization shows temperature changes across major U.S. cities from 1985 to 2025. Each circle marker represents a city, and the color reflects the magnitude of temperature increase.

Warmer colors indicate larger increases in temperature, which may suggest regional climate trends.

Notes

This dataset is simplified for demonstration purposes and does not include full climate variability.
